CompoDynamics is a comprehensive database of molecular composition dynamics of genes and genomes and represents 36,000+ latest genomes and over 127.7 million coding sequences. CompoDynamics annotates multiple molecular composition features including nucleotide composition, synonymous codon composition, codon usage, amino acid usage, codon usage bias, coding potential and protein physicochemical properties.


Examples:  Fungi; Homo sapiens; NC_000001.11; hypothetical protein;
